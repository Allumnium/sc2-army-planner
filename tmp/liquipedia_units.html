Key[edit]

  • / / - Amount of Psi/Supply/Control.
  • - Mineral warp/build/morph cost.
  • / / - Gas warp/build/morph cost.
  • / / - Time needed to warp/build/morph.
  • - Amount of Hit Points (upgrade bonus in brackets).
  • - Amount of Shields.
  • - Amount of default Armor (upgrade bonus in brackets).
  • A. Attack - Air Attack (upgrade bonus in brackets).
  • A. DPS - Air Damage Per Second (upgrade bonus in brackets).
  • Bonus - Amount of bonus damage versus Type.
  • Bonus DPS - Amount of bonus damage per second versus Type.
  • Cargo - Cargo space used in Warp Prisms/Bunkers/Medivacs/Overlords.
  • Cooldown - Attack cooldown (value after upgrade in brackets).
  • G. Attack - Ground Attack (upgrade bonus in brackets).
  • G. DPS - Ground Damage Per Second (upgrade bonus in brackets).
  • Range - Attack range (upgrade bonus in brackets), M - Melee.
  • Sight - Sight range.
  • Size - Unit collision diameter or building footprint size. (note: units may have smaller diameters for collision with structures, air units can overlap when moving).
  • Speed - Movement speed (upgrade bonus in brackets).
